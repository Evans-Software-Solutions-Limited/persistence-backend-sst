# Product Steering

## Working thesis

People do not just need workout plans. They need a coach that helps them keep going when life gets messy.

So the product should focus on:

- adherence
- practical nutrition support
- clear next steps
- honest progress review
- emotional momentum without cringe

## Primary persona

Busy adult, likely training inconsistently, wants visible progress and structure, but does not want bodybuilding-level complexity.

## Core pains

- doesn't know what matters most
- falls off routine easily
- overthinks nutrition
- wants feedback between PT sessions or without a PT
- has data, but not clarity

## Promise

A coach that helps you train, eat, and stay on track like someone competent is actually paying attention.

## Non-goals

- medical diagnosis
- advanced sports science for elite athletes
- trying to replace a clinician
- trying to do everything a full fitness platform does on day one
