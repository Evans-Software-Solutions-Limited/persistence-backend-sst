# Open Food Facts attribution (ODbL)

`off-uk.jsonl.gz` is a curated UK subset derived from the
[Open Food Facts](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/) product database.

- **Source:** Open Food Facts, https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data
- **Licence:** Open Database License (ODbL) v1.0 — https://opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/1-0/
- **Database contents** (individual facts) are under the Database Contents Licence (DbCL) v1.0.

Open Food Facts data is made available under the ODbL. Any redistribution of this
subset (including via this repository) must keep this attribution and remain under
the ODbL/DbCL. In-app, foods sourced from OFF are tagged `source='openfoodfacts'`
and attribution is surfaced to users.

This file is a derivative database (filtered to UK products with complete macros,
deduped by barcode). It is **not** the full Open Food Facts database.
